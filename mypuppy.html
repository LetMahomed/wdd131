Carminha and Pipo

Hello! I'm Leticia, and I'd love to introduce you to my adorable puppies, Pipo and Carminha. Although Pipo sadly passed away in 2022, he left a lasting impression with his boundless energy and incredible spirit. He is the one that is mostly white. Despite being deaf, he was remarkably attentive and aware of everything happening around him. On the other hand, Carminha has way more spots being mostly black. Carminha has a distinct personality. She is calm, obedient, and enjoys cozy days watching TikTok videos with me. However, she has a playful side and loves to hunt, her favorite hobby, especially for bones. Known as the 'cow puppy' she is classy and elegant, Carminha adores hugs and kisses and never misses a chance to run around after other puppies and chats.
